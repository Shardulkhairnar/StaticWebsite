Our Top Recommendations

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is not just about forts and palaces but it also has a beautiful culture, amazing food, silver jewels and more. It is the kind of destination that combines adventure and relaxation and makes for an unforgettable holiday..

Gujrat

Gujrat is renowned for its beaches, temple towns and historic capitals. Wildlife sanctuaries, hill resorts and natural grandeur are gifts of Gujarat. Sculpture, handicrafts, arts, festivals also make the state rich.

Punjab

Punjab is known for numerous reasons. It is known as 'The land of five rivers' and because of the fertile lands found here, it is also amongst one of the most ancient civilizations in the world. This place is rich with culture and full of beauty that you should experience first-hand.
